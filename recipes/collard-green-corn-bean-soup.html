Ingredients

  • Yellow Onion
  • Corn Kernels
  • Collard Greens
  • Tomato Paste
  • Beans (Pinto)
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium-low heat.
  2. Add the onions and salt and cook until the onion is almost translucent, about 8 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato paste and red pepper flakes and saute until the onions are coated with the paste and begin to wilt, about 5 minutes
  4. Add 8 cups of water. Then add the corn and beans.
  5. Reduce the heat and cook partially covered at a low simmer until the beans and corn are almost tender, about 15 to 20 minutes if using canned items.
  6. Add the collard greens and cook at a simmer until the greens are slightly tender and are still a lighter green. Too dark and will being to turn to mush.
